Lead Generator

RoleStaff Designer
IndustryDeveloper SaaS
Timeline4 months
Reading time6 min
Overview Problem Design Details Results

Overview

Lead Generator is an AI-powered enterprise platform designed to help sales and recruitment teams discover, qualify, and manage potential business leads from multiple data sources.

The goal was to reduce manual lead research by centralizing lead discovery, AI-assisted qualification, and pipeline management into a single workflow.

120ms

Maximum input response lag

Problem

Sales teams relied on multiple websites, spreadsheets, and CRM tools to identify and organize potential leads. Gathering company information, validating contacts, and tracking outreach required repetitive manual effort and resulted in fragmented workflows.

As lead volume increased, teams struggled to prioritize high-quality prospects, maintain accurate data, and collaborate efficiently across sales pipelines.

Design Details

The platform was designed around three core experiences:

  • AI Lead Discovery: Automatically identify and organize potential leads based on industry, location, company size, and business requirements.
  • Lead Qualification: Surface relevant company information, contact details, and qualification insights to help sales teams prioritize opportunities.
  • Pipeline Management: Provide a centralized dashboard for tracking lead status, assignments, outreach progress, and follow-up activities.

This approach simplified lead management while enabling teams to focus on meaningful sales conversations instead of manual data collection.

Results

After implementing the Lead Generator platform, sales teams reported significant improvements in lead management efficiency and data accuracy. The AI-assisted workflows reduced manual research time, allowing teams to focus on high-value interactions with prospects.
