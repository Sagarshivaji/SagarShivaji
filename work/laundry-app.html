Overview

Provide a transparent and effortless on‑demand laundry experience with real‑time order updates.

Users find traditional laundry services inconvenient because they cannot track order status, pricing isn't transparent, scheduling is confusing, and existing apps are either outdated or cluttered.

5

User Surveys Conducted

The Core Challenge

Traditional laundry services fail to meet modern expectations. Users are left in the dark, confused by pricing, and frustrated with scheduling.

  • No visibility — Users have no way to track their order once it leaves their doorstep.
  • Hidden costs — Pricing is often unclear, leading to surprise charges and distrust.
  • Confusing scheduling — Pick‑up and delivery windows are vague, causing missed appointments.
  • Cluttered apps — Existing apps are overloaded with features, making the core task hard to find.

Understanding the Landscape

To understand the current laundry experience and identify UX gaps, I carried out user surveys and competitor analysis.

What I Learned

01

Users value visibility above all else — they want to know where their laundry is at every step.

02

Transparent pricing is the #1 trust signal. Hidden fees are the fastest way to lose a customer.

03

Scheduling flexibility matters more than speed. Users want to choose when pick‑up and delivery happen.

04

A clean, focused interface wins. Users don't want a Swiss Army knife — they want a simple tool that works.

Learnings

1

Research is non‑negotiable. The survey and competitor analysis revealed gaps we would have missed otherwise.

2

Simplicity scales. A clean interface isn't just aesthetic — it reduces cognitive load and speeds up conversion.

3

Transparency builds trust. Users are willing to pay more when they know exactly what they're getting.

4

Guest access removes friction. Forcing sign‑up is the #1 reason users abandon a service.
